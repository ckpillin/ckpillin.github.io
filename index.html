Some notes about hiking safely in Arizona

  • Watch the Weather! Arizona's temperature can be deceiving and deadly.
  • Watch for flash flood warnings. They can happen quickly and without warning!
  • Dress Appropriately & Wear Sunscreen.
  • Bring Lots of Water. Take more water than you think you'll need!
  • Carry a mobile phone.
  • Hike with others.
  • Be honest about your ability level!
  • Don't Trailblaze!
  • Be the responsible hiker who takes a hike and does it right.
